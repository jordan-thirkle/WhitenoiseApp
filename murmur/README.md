# Murmur

**Premium Ambient Soundscape Utility for Families**

Murmur is a high-performance ambient sound mixer designed for 2026+ mobile ecosystems. It replaces standard noise apps with a multi-track "Liquid Glass" mixing engine, optimized for long-duration background playback and thermal stability.

## Features

- **Liquid Glass UI**: A state-of-the-art, dark-theme interface with real-time glassmorphic effects and premium animations.
- **Multi-Track Mixer**: Independently control 10+ high-fidelity ambient tracks (Rain, Heartbeat, White Noise, Fan, etc.) to create your perfect sleep environment.
- **Thermal Intelligence**: Built-in monitoring ensures the app remains within optimal thermal envelopes for 8+ hour sleep sessions.
- **HealthKit Integration**: Seamlessly sync sleep duration data to Apple Health.
- **Offline Sovereignty**: Works 100% offline and in Airplane Mode—zero data leaves your device.
- **Built for Families**: Zero-data compliance (COPPA/GDPR-K aligned) and kid-safe background playback.

## Technical Stack

- **Framework**: Flutter (Dart)
- **Audio Engine**: Just Audio + Audio Service (Optimized OGG Loops)
- **State Management**: Riverpod
- **Architecture**: Feature-first structure with local compute priority.
